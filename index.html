Hacknation 2021

Learn to code.

Hacknation is a PAN India program focused on bringing more student developers into project
based learning. Students work with tech organizations on a 12 week programming
project during their summer vacations.

About Us

Hacknation is an initiative to build a community for tech-enthusiasts
who have the potential to create
a strong base in Software Programming/ Development and
Engineering and are driven towards coding.
Our aim is to bring all the Undergrad and Grad scholars
to a platform through events and enhance the competitive
capabilities of students + the content on the existing website

Timeline

  • Jan 17 2021 -

    Application open -

    Companies and institutions will register themselves to mentor students enrolled in the program

  • Feb 28 2021

    Application close

    All the applications post Feb 28, 2021 will be considered for the next flagship event.

  • March 8 2021

    Meet the mentor

    Applicants will present their project ideas with the mentors

  • March 8 2021

    Registration process

    Send your proposal to the mentor

  • March 12 2021

    Selection process

  • April 15 2021

    Project announcement

    All the matched mentor student teams will decide their project roadmap

  • May 1 2021

    Project based working

    Applicants will start coding their project ideas

  • July 1 2021

    Project Submission

    Project roadmap is a must, to finalize and evaluate your project on time

  • September 15 2021

    Results

    Results will be announced post evaluation the cetificate will mark on pass/fail basis
